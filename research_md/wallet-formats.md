## Most Popular Crypto Wallet Address Formats in 2025-2026

Based on recent data from 2025-2026, the most popular wallet address formats correspond to leading cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and others. Formats have remained stable, with increased adoption of efficient ones like Bech32 for Bitcoin. Popularity is driven by market cap, transaction volume, and ecosystem growth (e.g., Solana's speed and Cardano's upgrades).

| Cryptocurrency | Format Type | Prefix | Description/Example |
|---------------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| Bitcoin (BTC) | Legacy (P2PKH) | 1 | Base58-encoded, 26-35 characters. Example: 1A1zP1eP5QGefi2DMPTfTL5SLmv7DivfNa |
| Bitcoin (BTC) | Script (P2SH) | 3 | Base58-encoded for scripts. Example: 3J98t1WpEZ73CNmQviecrnyiWrnqRhWNLy |
| Bitcoin (BTC) | SegWit (Bech32) | bc1q | Native SegWit, most common in 2026 for lower fees. Example: bc1qar0srrr7xfkvy5l643lydnw9re59gtzzwf5mdq |
| Bitcoin (BTC) | Taproot (P2TR) | bc1p | Advanced privacy features, gaining traction. Example: bc1pmfr3p9j00pfxjh0z0r2pmuyyqg2y4c2mpepr54nma3w4mfs8p9ssdhkd0h |
| Ethereum (ETH) | Hexadecimal | 0x | 42 characters (including prefix). Example: 0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc454e4438f44e |
| Solana (SOL) | Base58 | None (alphanumeric start) | 32-44 characters. Example: 5FHwkndxJ3eNvh5D56ga2vN6NWX2fQY9a3tJ5GZf8p7J |
| Cardano (ADA) | Shelley (Bech32) | addr1 | Modern format, 58-64 characters. Example: addr1qx2fxv2umyhttfhnuispn5e6hwrh4z7eax8q6x4gv2rcgu |
| Tron (TRX) | Base58 | T | 34 characters. Example: TAJA5XbXnD7oYa6V7pV4uH3uF3Gf1v8o3K |
| Ripple (XRP) | Base58 | r | 25-35 characters. Example: rHb9CJAWyB4rj91VRWn96DkukG4bwdtyTh |
| Litecoin (LTC) | Base58 | L or M | Similar to Bitcoin Legacy. Example: LTub2kWbYUaG4aFsv1xJWMRAJcdY4UgN46 |
