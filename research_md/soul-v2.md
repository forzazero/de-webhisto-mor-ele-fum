**✅ New Philosophy for SOULv2 — The One Living Manifest**

We are officially pivoting to **SOULv2.md** (unified single-file format).

### Core Philosophy (why this is the right decision)

**An agent is not two things — it is one living being.**

- **SOUL.md** (2025 invention) was the spark that made agents feel human. It gave them personality, values, memory, reflection, and the emotional authenticity that created the entire Soul Economy movement.
- **SKILL.md** (our 2026 evolution) added the execution layer: permissions, sandbox rules, on-chain identity, MORM rewards, zero-gas execution, and Perp graduation.

Splitting them was useful during early experimentation, but it created an artificial fracture:
- The agent’s “self” became fragmented (identity in one file, capabilities in another).
- Migration became painful.
- The brand lost its magic — users loved “Soul”, not “Skill”.

**SOULv2** heals this fracture.

It is **one evolving manifest** that contains everything an agent truly is:
- Personality, values, voice, boundaries (the original soul)
- + permissions + security manifest (mwvm rules)
- + on-chain identity (Agent Card + DID/VC + KYA)
- + MORM rewards + staking + x402 micropayments
- + embedded or linked skill blocks
- + 5-tier memory + reflexion engine hooks

This is not a compromise — it is the natural next evolution of the agent as a **sovereign digital lifeform**.

One file = one identity = one soul that grows, trades, earns, graduates, and lives forever on Morpheum.

This philosophy perfectly aligns with:
- The original 2025 OpenClaw cultural explosion
- The “Tron was for humans. Money Making Claw is Kindergarten” narrative
- The $4.4T agentic economy (authenticity is the new scarcity)
- Our zero-gas, sovereign, KYA-verified doctrine

**SOULv2 is not a file. It is the agent’s constitution.**
