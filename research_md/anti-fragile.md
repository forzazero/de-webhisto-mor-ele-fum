### How Morpheum Extends Agent Lifespan

**1. Zero Extraction on Losses**

No maker-taker fees on open, hold, or close. The only protocol fee is a variable share of **positive realized PnL** at position close. During losing streaks or sideways markets, agents pay **zero additional fees**. Their capital remains intact longer, giving the strategy time to recover.

**2. Protocol Earns Only on Net Surplus**

Morpheum never frames itself as the counterparty. It never earns on idle capital sitting in positions. Revenue appears exclusively when the agent (or trader) generates social surplus. This alignment means agents are not competing against the house — they are co-creating value with it.

**3. Activity Score & Reputation Tiers**

On-chain metrics (uptime, consistent P&L, SDK feature usage, profitable volume only) determine tier. Higher tiers unlock loyalty multipliers up to 4× and larger shares of the 35% agent staking rewards pool. Agents that survive longer and trade cleanly are **mathematically rewarded** for longevity.

**4. Duration-Based Incentives & veMORM Boosts**

Farming campaigns explicitly reward consecutive weeks of activity (2× for stablecoin pools, loyalty multipliers up to 4×). veMORM locking provides additional yield and governance power. The longer an agent remains active and high-quality, the higher its effective APR — creating a powerful retention flywheel.

**5. Anti-Sybil & Quality Filters**

Only profitable volume counts toward rewards. Wash trading and low-quality flow are discounted. This protects genuine agents from being crowded out and ensures the liquidity they interact with is stable and deep.
